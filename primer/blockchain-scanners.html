What Are Blockchain Scanners?

Blockchain ledger scanners are special websites that let you look up what’s happening on a blockchain, kind of like searching for a package with a tracking number. You can use them to see when transactions happen, who sent or received something, and even check if a digital item or game asset really exists.

For example, if you buy or earn something in a game that uses blockchain, you can use these scanners to find and verify your transaction. This helps you know your stuff is real and keeps everything transparent—no secret changes or hidden tricks. Even if you’re not a tech expert, these tools make it easy to see what’s going on behind the scenes!
